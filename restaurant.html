Restaurant Description

The Bestaurant opened its doors in 1995 and provides beachgoes the option to eat simple homestyle food while partaking in the breathtaking views of the beach. Our mission statment is to provide the best homecooked food for the cheapest prices. Eating while vacationing in paradise shouldnt drain your wallet so we also offer free food for children under 12.
